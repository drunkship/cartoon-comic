Create your Private Paradise

Open Every Day by Appointment - (805)712-1759

Ask about our - Farm and Nursery Tour

We take great pride in growing some very special bamboos to allow you to create your private paradise right in your home. A place where you can come home and enjoy a peaceful and relaxing private paradise to unwind after a hard day's work or to share and enjoy with your family and friends.

Bamboo is unique in creating a calming and relaxing environment. It's tall stature and magnificently green culms and leaves create an outdoor ambient unlike any other plant.

Visit us, tour our full size bamboo groves, feel the cooling and calming effects and begin to imagine your private paradise. Whether you are looking for a beautiful green privacy screen to sorround your yard, add some bamboos for a tropical effect or a large bamboo specimen to highlight a key garden area, we have it. From shrub size to giant timbers, we grow them.

What We Do

We'll make sure you'll get the best bamboo for your Private Paradise.

 We specialize in very hardy, easy to grow bamboos, that will thrive in your garden. Our bamboos were selected for their cold hardiness, heat resistance and their special beauty. We experience winters in the teens and summers that peak around 120F. Our selected bamboos grow very well here and will actually do better if you have milder conditions.

We carry plants in 5gal, 15gal and 25gal or dug to order. Review our site, but it's best if you stop by, tour our farm and let us know what your plans are.

You can hand select the plants which best suit you. We are in San Luis Obispo (slo) County, the belly button of California.
