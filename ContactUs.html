Create your own Paradise!

I encourage your visit to our farm/nursery or call to ask any questions you might have about bamboo or anything related, you'll most likely be talking to me.

Presently we are open by appointment, I am working most of the time in our groves, so if you are in the area, and would like to visit, please call us and we most likely will be able to accomodate you.

We have served clients from the San Francisco Bay area to Los Angeles and East to the Great Valley. Just about every town in between, San Jose, Redwood City, Los Banos, Salinas, King City, Tulare,Fresno, San Miguel, Templeton, Atascadero, Pismo, Arroyo Grande, Nipomo, Cambria, Solvang, Santa Barbara and Glendale. We would love to serve your town.

Our bamboo farm is about 5 miles north of Paso Robles, just 1 mile east of Highway 101. We are at 5590 North River Road which runs parallel to 101 and goes from Paso Robles to San Miguel.
